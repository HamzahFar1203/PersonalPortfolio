About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website. My passion for programming began when I took a course in high school about the basics of Web Development. I took the class hoping to start an exciting journey in the world of programming, and that's exactly what happened. Although my time in that particular class did not prove to be fruitful, It introduced me to something that I would have a never-ending passion for, programming. Although I was presented with many career choices under the category of "programming", I chose Web Development because, in my opinion, it's the best of all worlds. Web development allows me to be creative; it allows me to take my ideas and turn them into interactive experiences for others to see; because of this, I am always excited to learn and implement new technologies that allow me to make better websites/experiences.

My main goal is to become a Front End Web Developer, and I'm willing to learn whatever I need to learn to reach that goal.
